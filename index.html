Product

A clear system for day-to-day operations

Achika brings together the parts of the workflow that usually end up scattered across spreadsheets, messages, and memory.

Orders

Keep all orders in one place, track their status, and make it easier for the team to see what needs attention.

Inventory

See what is in stock, what is running low, and what is expected from incoming supply.

Fulfillment

Track what is being sourced, produced, picked, and prepared for delivery without constant manual follow-up.

Why it becomes useful

When order volume grows, spreadsheets become harder to manage

Orders get lost or delayed

The workflow becomes harder to track when information is spread across multiple places.

Stock is unclear

It becomes difficult to know what is available right now and what still needs to arrive.

Knowledge is concentrated

Too much depends on one person remembering the current state of orders and inventory.

Status questions never stop

The team keeps asking for updates because there is no single place to check progress.

Who it is for

Built for small teams managing physical goods

  • Small manufacturers and distributors with 2–10 employees
  • Teams managing orders in spreadsheets, email, or chat
  • Businesses with growing order volume and a repeatable workflow
  • Companies not ready for a complex ERP implementation

Simple by design

A practical system, not an enterprise project

No ERP-level complexity.

No long implementation process.

Start quickly with your existing workflow and get visibility into orders, stock, and fulfillment in one place.
